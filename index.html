My Skills & Achievements:

This section would have a brief description of each of my achievements:-

  • Regional Mathematical Olympiad is the second stage in the Mathematical Olympiad programme which leads to the selection of students from India for the participation in the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO). My achievement was the qualification in RMO for the third stage, which is INMO (Indian National Mathematical Olympiad).

  • Codechef is a well known Competitive Programming website that offers an ample amount of Questions to learn and practice Competitive Programming. Codechef hosts a lot of contests based on which it gives a star-count to each user. My star-count currently in Codechef is 3*.

  • Indian Olympiad Qualifier in Chemistry (IOQC) consists of the first two stages for the selection of students from India for the International Chemistry Olympiad. My achievement was that Qualified for the second stage of the Chemistry Olympiad Programme and secured the top 1% merit in the State.

  • Cambridge English is one of the most popular exams, accepted by thousands of businesses and educational institutions worldwide. A B2 First qualification proves that one has the language skills to live and work independently in an English-speaking country or study courses taught in English. My achievement was that I successfully secured the B2 First (FCE) certificate.

  • Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY) is an on-going National Program of Fellowship in Basic Sciences, initiated and funded by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, to attract exceptionally highly motivated students for pursuing basic science courses and research career in science. My achievement was that I secured Rank 1096 in the general category and thereby being eligible for the fellowship.

  • Dr. Homi Bhabha Balvaidnyanik Competition is conducted by the Greater Bombay Science Teachers' Association since 1981. This unique four stage competition aims at arousing curiosity, problem solving attitude along with development of precision in observational and motor skills in young scientists. Through the competition, GBSTA also aims at sensitizing students towards their immediate environment and dare to take rational initiatives whenever needed. I cleared all the stages of this competition and secured the bronze medal.
